PROCESS

We thought about the bags’ aesthetic quality as well as their practicality. A handbag should be elegant, beautiful and useful.

Our handbags are made from highest quality Italian leather. The designs employ different textures, patterns and shades. The leather is embossed in python and crocodile skin patterns. While designing, we focus on the aesthetic and pragmatic aspects of our products: a purse should be elegant, pretty, and useful.

The bags are manufactured in Poland, which allows to achieve highest quality. The collections are relased in limited series, with every bag having its series numer hand-engraved.
